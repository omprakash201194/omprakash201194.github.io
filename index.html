About me

Distributed systems guy who loves a hard problem.

I'm a Lead Backend Engineer at Qualys, where I lead middleware architecture for the Enterprise TruRisk Management (ETM) platform — building 8–10 Spring Boot microservices from the ground up and mentoring a team of 8 engineers.

My sweet spot is the intersection of scale, reliability and security. I've spent years working on multi-tenant SaaS platforms, Kafka-based event pipelines, Elasticsearch-heavy search frameworks, and Redis-backed distributed caching systems.

Outside of work I write about engineering on my blog and occasionally publish research on distributed computing.
